Our Technology

AI-powered acoustic analysis for instant reef diagnostics

Built on SurfPerch

Acoustic Reef is powered by SurfPerch, a state-of-the-art deep learning model developed by Google Research for marine bioacoustics.

SurfPerch uses a convolutional neural network architecture to learn acoustic representations from millions of underwater recordings. It transforms raw audio into rich embeddings that capture:

  • Spectral patterns of fish vocalizations
  • Temporal dynamics of biological choruses
  • Acoustic signatures of invertebrate activity
  • Environmental noise characteristics

We fine-tuned SurfPerch on a curated dataset of healthy and degraded reef recordings, achieving 95% classification accuracy‚Äîa significant improvement over traditional acoustic indices.

How Acoustic Reef Works

From audio file to actionable insights in under 5 seconds

01

Audio Upload

Users upload a WAV or MP3 file via our Streamlit web interface. We accept recordings of any duration, though 30-60 seconds is optimal.

Formats: WAV, MP3, FLAC Duration: 10s - 5min Sample Rate: Any
‚Üì
02

Audio Preprocessing

The audio is resampled to 32kHz and converted to a mel-spectrogram‚Äîa time-frequency representation that highlights biologically relevant acoustic features.

Resample: 32kHz Window: 1024 samples Mel bins: 128
‚Üì
03

SurfPerch Embedding

The spectrogram passes through SurfPerch's convolutional layers, producing a 1024-dimensional embedding vector that encodes the acoustic signature.

Model: SurfPerch CNN Embedding: 1024-dim Time: ~2 seconds
‚Üì
04

Classification & Scoring

A trained Random Forest classifier processes the embedding to predict reef health (Healthy/Degraded) and compute a biophony score (0-10).

Classifier: Random Forest Accuracy: 95% Confidence: Provided
‚Üì
05

UMAP Visualization

Your recording is projected into a 2D UMAP space alongside global reef recordings, revealing acoustic similarity to healthy and degraded ecosystems worldwide.

Method: UMAP Neighbors: 15 Min dist: 0.1
‚Üì
06

Results Dashboard

All results are displayed in an interactive dashboard: health classification, confidence score, biophony level, spectrogram, and UMAP plot.

Delivery: Instant Format: Interactive Export: Available

Innovation & Impact

Acoustic Reef democratizes marine conservation. By making reef health assessment instant, free, and accessible to anyone with a hydrophone, we're removing barriers that have historically limited monitoring to well-funded institutions.

Our key innovations include:

  • Speed: Analysis in seconds vs. weeks of manual processing
  • Accessibility: Free web app vs. expensive specialized software
  • Simplicity: No marine biology expertise required
  • Scalability: Can process thousands of recordings simultaneously
  • Global Context: UMAP comparison to worldwide reef acoustics

We're empowering coastal communities, citizen scientists, and under-resourced conservation organizations to monitor their reefs with the same tools available to major research institutions.
