Our Vision

Democratizing coral reef health assessment through accessible technology

A World Where Anyone Can Monitor Reefs

Until recently, coral reef monitoring required expensive dive teams and weeks of data processing. Assessment from an acoustic and AI angle is complementary to visual surveys and offers vast democratization potential.
