Our Technology

AI and bioacoustics dedicated to reef health assessment

Our solution combines the latest AI models, acoustic signal processing, and machine learning to deliver instant reef diagnostics.

  • Analysis in under 5 seconds from any audio recording
  • SurfPerch-based deep learning model trained on global reef datasets
  • Non-intrusive monitoring with no equipment deployment required
  • UMAP visualization showing acoustic similarity across reefs worldwide
